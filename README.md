# customer-churn prediction
Customer churn prediction system

Customer Churn Prediction is a data analytics and machine learning solution that helps businesses identify customers who are likely to stop using their products or services. By analyzing customer behavior, purchase history, service usage, and engagement patterns, the system predicts the probability of customer churn and enables organizations to take proactive retention measures.

The model uses historical customer data to detect patterns associated with churn, helping companies understand the key factors influencing customer departures. Businesses can then implement targeted marketing campaigns, personalized offers, and customer support strategies to improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.

Identification of Churn Factors
